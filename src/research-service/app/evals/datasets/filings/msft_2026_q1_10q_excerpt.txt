Item 2. Management's Discussion and Analysis of Financial Condition and Results of Operations
Cloud revenue increased as Azure consumption grew and commercial bookings remained healthy.
Operating income expanded because productivity software demand and cloud gross margin improved.
Management discussed outlook for AI infrastructure investment and enterprise demand.

Segment Information
Productivity and Business Processes benefited from Microsoft 365 commercial seat growth.
Intelligent Cloud growth was driven by Azure, server products, and enterprise services.
More Personal Computing included Windows, devices, gaming, and search advertising.

Liquidity and Capital Resources
Cash provided by operations funded capital expenditures for data centers and share repurchases.
Liquidity was supported by cash, short-term investments, and access to credit markets.
