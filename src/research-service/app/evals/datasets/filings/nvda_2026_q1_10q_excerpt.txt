Item 2. Management's Discussion and Analysis of Financial Condition and Results of Operations
Revenue increased because data center demand for accelerated computing and networking products grew.
Gross margin benefited from product mix, higher volume, and disciplined cost management.
Management discussed supply availability, customer demand, and investments in new products.

Segment Information
Data Center revenue reflected compute, networking, and AI infrastructure demand.
Gaming revenue reflected GPU sell-through, channel inventory, and product transitions.
Professional Visualization and Automotive revenue contributed to diversified growth.

Liquidity and Capital Resources
Cash provided by operating activities supported inventory purchases, capital expenditures, and share repurchases.
Liquidity remained strong due to cash, investments, and operating cash generation.

Item 1A. Risk Factors
Export controls, supply constraints, customer concentration, competition, and product transition risk could affect results.
