Item 2. Management's Discussion and Analysis of Financial Condition and Results of Operations
Net sales increased because AWS growth, advertising revenue, and retail demand improved.
Operating income expanded due to fulfillment productivity, cloud margin, and cost discipline.
Management discussed investment in logistics, AI infrastructure, and customer experience.

Segment Information
North America revenue reflected online stores, third-party seller services, advertising, and subscriptions.
AWS revenue increased due to customer usage, enterprise migration, and cloud services demand.
International results reflected foreign exchange, retail demand, and regional cost structure.

Liquidity and Capital Resources
Operating cash flow funded capital expenditures for fulfillment centers, technology infrastructure, and content.
Liquidity was supported by cash, marketable securities, and access to debt markets.

Item 1A. Risk Factors
Competition, cloud demand, regulatory scrutiny, fulfillment execution, and foreign exchange could affect results.
