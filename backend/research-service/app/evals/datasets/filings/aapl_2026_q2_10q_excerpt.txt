Item 2. Management's Discussion and Analysis of Financial Condition and Results of Operations
Net sales increased because Services revenue grew and iPhone demand improved in several markets.
Gross margin benefited from a favorable mix of Services and disciplined cost of sales.
Management's outlook discussed stable demand expectations and continued investment in products.

Segment Information
The company discussed performance across iPhone, Mac, iPad, Wearables, and Services.
Services growth reflected installed base engagement, subscriptions, and higher advertising revenue.

Liquidity and Capital Resources
Net cash provided by operating activities funded capital expenditures and repurchases of common stock.
Liquidity remained strong while debt maturities remained manageable.

Item 1A. Risk Factors
Competition, supply constraints, regulation, and foreign exchange volatility could affect future results.
