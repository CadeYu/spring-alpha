Item 2. Management's Discussion and Analysis of Financial Condition and Results of Operations
Automotive revenue declined because average selling prices decreased and delivery mix changed.
Gross margin was affected by pricing actions, manufacturing costs, and ramp expenses.
Management discussed demand generation, production efficiency, and energy storage growth.

Segment Information
Automotive results reflected vehicle deliveries, regulatory credits, and leasing revenue.
Energy generation and storage revenue increased due to deployments of storage products.

Liquidity and Capital Resources
Operating cash flow funded capital expenditures for manufacturing capacity and product development.
The company maintained liquidity through cash balances and financing flexibility.
